Classic Beef Cheeseburger

The Classic Beef Cheeseburger is a renowned American sandwich consisting of a seasoned, grilled or pan-fried beef patty topped with melted cheese (typically Cheddar or American), served inside a toasted hamburger bun, often accompanied by various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, and favorite sauces.

Ingredients & Instructions

  • Pattie (Meat)

    • Ground Beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
    • Salt and Pepper
    • Olive Oil (or neutral oil)
    • Sliced Cheese (Cheddar or American)

  • Buns and Toppings

    • Hamburger Buns
    • Lettuce Leaves
    • Tomato
    • Red Onion
    • Dill Pickles (Optional)

  • Sauces

    • Ketchup, Mustard, Mayonnaise

  1. Preparing the Pattie

    • Seasoning: In a medium bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Do not overmix the meat, as this will result in a tough burger.
    • Divide: Divide the seasoned beef into four (4) equal portions.
    • Form: Gently form each portion into a patty about 1 inch thick and slightly wider than your bun.
    • Create a Dimple: Use your thumb to create a small indent (dimple) in the center of each patty. This prevents the burger from rounding up while cooking.

  2. Cooking

    • Preheat Pan/Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat, or heat the olive oil in a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Cook/Grill: Place the patties on the hot grill or pan.
      • Medium Doneness: Cook for 4-5 minutes per side.
      • Well-done: Cook for 6-7 minutes per side.
      • Do not press down on the burgers while cooking to keep the juices inside.
    • Cheese: During the last 1 minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on top of each patty. Allow it to melt.
    • Toast Buns: While the burgers rest briefly, lightly toast the cut-sides of the buns on the grill or in the pan for about 1 minute.

  3. Assembly and Serving

    • Sauce: Spread your chosen sauces (mayo, ketchup, mustard) on the inside of both the top and bottom bun halves.
    • Assemble:
      • Place the Lettuce on the bottom bun.
      • Add the Tomato and Red Onion slices.
      • Place the Cheeseburger Patty.
      • Add Dill Pickles (if using).
      • Cover with the top bun.
    • Serve: Serve immediately while hot!
